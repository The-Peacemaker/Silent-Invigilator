How It Works

From camera feed to alert generation — a seamless pipeline of AI-driven analysis.

01

02

Analyze

YOLOv8 + MediaPipe process each frame — detecting faces, objects, gaze direction, and head pose.

03

04

Instant notifications to invigilators when risk exceeds threshold with detailed evidence logs.
